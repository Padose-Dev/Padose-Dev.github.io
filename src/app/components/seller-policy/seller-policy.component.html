Seller Policy – Prohibited & Restricted Products

Effective Date: {{ lastUpdated }}

Padose is committed to maintaining a safe, lawful, and trustworthy marketplace for buyers and sellers. All sellers on the Padose platform must comply with the following policy regarding prohibited and restricted products.

Listing, advertising, or selling any prohibited product on Padose is strictly forbidden and will result in enforcement action.

1

Prohibited Products

The following categories of products are strictly prohibited from being listed, advertised, or sold on Padose:

  • Alcohol & Alcoholic Beverages – Including but not limited to beer, wine, spirits, and any alcohol-based consumable products.
  • Tobacco & Related Products – Including cigarettes, cigars, chewing tobacco, e-cigarettes, vapes, and related accessories.
  • Weapons & Ammunition – Including firearms, knives (beyond household utility), explosives, pellet guns, and any item designed to cause harm.
  • Drugs & Controlled Substances – Including narcotics, psychotropic substances, prescription medications (without valid licence), and drug paraphernalia.
  • Counterfeit & Pirated Goods – Products that infringe on trademarks, copyrights, or intellectual property rights.
  • Hazardous Materials – Chemicals, flammable substances, or any materials classified as dangerous goods under applicable law.
  • Adult & Obscene Content – Sexually explicit material or products that violate public decency standards.
  • Stolen or Illegally Obtained Goods – Any product that lacks legitimate proof of ownership or origin.
  • Wildlife & Protected Species Products – Items derived from endangered or protected animals and plants.
2

Seller Obligations

By listing products on Padose, sellers agree to:

  • Ensure all listed products comply with applicable central and state laws of India.
  • Provide accurate and truthful product descriptions, images, and pricing.
  • Obtain and maintain all necessary licences, permits, and certifications required for the products they sell.
  • Promptly remove any product flagged by Padose for potential policy violation.
3

Consequences of Violation

Padose reserves the right to take the following actions against sellers found in violation of this policy:

  • Immediate removal of the offending product listing(s).
  • Suspension of the seller's account pending review.
  • Permanent termination of the seller's account for repeated or severe violations.
  • Withholding of pending payments related to transactions involving prohibited products.
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities where the violation involves illegal activity.

Padose may act on violations reported by buyers, identified through internal review, or flagged by regulatory authorities. Decisions taken by Padose under this policy are final and binding.

4

Reporting Violations

If you come across a product listing that you believe violates this policy, please report it to us at support@padose.com.

5

Governing Law

This Seller Policy is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising from this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Rajahmundry (Urban), ANDHRA PRADESH.
